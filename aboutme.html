about me

From New York City, I currently attend Cornell University as an Information Science major, on the Pre-Medicine track.

Interested in both medicine and biotechnology, I have experience in research and laboratory work. I currently work in the Cognitive Neuroscience Lab at Cornell and as a research intern in the laryngology department of Weill Cornell of Medicine. I am currently working with an larynologist on developing a cost-effective medical device for patients in low-resource countries.

I design with purpose. I have experience with InDesign, Photoshop, Lightroom, and Illustrator. Within the Information Science major, I plan on concentrating in user experience.
